HI-SEAS VR

Built the foundations of a simulation for an analog habitat for Human Exploration Operations (HEO) training. Work included fully modelling and texturing martian and lunar environments from Lunar Reconnaisance Orbiter (LRO) data and Hawaiian mountain topography, along with programming functionality for VR support, vehicle control, and geological tools (LIDAR, XRF).

Built as part of an internship with NASA Goddard Space Flight Center (GSFC).
